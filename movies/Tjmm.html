Tu Jhooti Main Makkaar (Film)

Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar, abbreviated as TJMM, is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language romantic comedy film directed by Luv Ranjan and written by Rahul Mody, Luv Ranjan. It is produced by Luv Films and T-Series Films starring Ranbir Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor, with Anubhav Singh Bassi, Dimple Kapadia and Boney Kapoor in supporting roles.

The film was theatrically released on 8 March 2023, coinciding with the Holi weekend. The film received positive reviews from critics praising the direction, performances, humour, soundtrack, and cinematography. It also marked Shraddha Kapoor's return to the big screen after three years.
